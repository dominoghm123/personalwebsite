This is 漫游者说theroversays, a solo podcast centred around travel narratives yet not fixated on destinations.
My guiding principle is this—"To nurture imagination for the unfamiliar; our wealth lies in observation."
Host Domino is an urban wanderer who also delights in navigating cyberspace. A key life goal is to expand the online map of Earth, uncovering more facets of the world and listening to its myriad voices.
The podcast is available across major platforms including Xiao Yu Zhou, Apple Podcasts, Spotify, NetEase Cloud Music, QQ Music, Lizhi, WeChat Video Account, and Weibo Audio.
